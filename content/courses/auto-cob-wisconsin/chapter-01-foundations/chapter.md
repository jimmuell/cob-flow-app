# Chapter 1 — Foundations: Why Auto COB Exists

**Spine:** Per `../COB_Flow_Auto_COB_Syllabus.docx` Module 1.

## Objective

Articulate why auto and health insurance overlap when someone is injured, and the cost of getting primacy wrong.

## Estimated study time

2–3 hours for the chapter (reading guide + summary + worked example + quiz).

## Prerequisites

- General health insurance vocabulary (premiums, deductibles, copays, coinsurance, claim processing, EOBs)
- General awareness of insurance types (PPO, HMO, POS plans)

No prior Auto COB exposure required.

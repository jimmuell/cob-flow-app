## Identifying the payers in play

<!-- TO BE AUTHORED — Jim
Target length: 300–400 words.
For this case, list every payer potentially on the hook for these medical bills:
1. Health plan (which one? what plan type?)
2. Auto med-pay / PIP (member's own auto policy)
3. At-fault liability (the other driver's BI coverage, if there's another driver)
4. Medicare or Medicaid (if applicable based on age / dual status)
5. Worker's comp (if injury occurred during work — usually not auto, but worth checking)

For each: a sentence on WHY it might be on the hook, and a sentence on what info you'd need to confirm.
-->
